Weekly Blog

WEEK 1

During my first week, I was assigned to scan the ID details of employees from the Katrangkiluhan Office, OPAG Office, PLO Office, and PTO Office. This task was done to collect and organize the ID information of Sorsogon Capitol employees for their respective office records and work assignments. Through this activity, I learned the importance of accuracy and attention to detail when handling employee data, as well as how proper documentation contributes to efficient office operations.

WEEK 2

During the second week, we conducted a feeding activity where we distributed lugaw to four barangays — Barangay Poblacion IV, Barangay Poblacion III, and other nearby barangays in Matnog, Sorsogon.

WEEK 3

During my third week, we joined a tree planting activity held in Barangay Hidhid, Barangay Bolo, and at an elementary school in Matnog, Sorsogon.

WEEK 4

During my fourth week, we scanned the ID details of employees from the Provincial Health Office (PHO), Kalusugan Office, Provincial Human Resource Management Office (PHRMO), and Provincial Engineering Office (PEO).

WEEK 5

During the fifth week, printing of identification cards for employees from the OPAG and PLO Offices was conducted. In addition, scanning activities were also carried out at the Katrangkiluhan Office for the processing of employee information.

WEEK 6

During the sixth week, an Excel database was created for employees from the Katrangkiluhan Office, Kalusugan Office, and OPAG Office.
